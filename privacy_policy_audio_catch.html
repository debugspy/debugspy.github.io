Privacy Policy for AudioCatch

At Debug Studio, we take your privacy seriously. AudioCatch is designed with a strong commitment to user privacy and data protection. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from our users.

Specifically, AudioCatch does not:

  • Collect any user data, including personal or usage information.
  • Upload any audio, video, or metadata to our servers.
  • Use analytics tools to track user behavior or app usage.
  • Include any form of third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

All media processing is performed locally on your device. None of your files leave your device at any point during conversion. Your content remains entirely private and under your control.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy practices, feel free to contact us at support@debugstudio.com.

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.
